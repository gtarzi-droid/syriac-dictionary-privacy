Privacy Policy — Syriac Dictionary

Effective date: July 6, 2026
App: Syriac Dictionary (iOS and Android)
Developer: George Tarzi
Contact: tarztech@gmail.com

Syriac Dictionary does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app works entirely on your device and needs no account, sign-in, or internet connection to use.

The app works offline

The dictionary, phrasebook, and pronunciation audio are bundled inside the app. Looking up a word or phrase runs entirely on your device — your searches are never sent anywhere, and no record of what you look up leaves your device.

Microphone and voice search

The app offers optional voice search in English and Arabic (there is no Syriac voice input — a note in the app explains why). When you tap the microphone:

  • Your speech is converted to text by your device's own operating system speech recognition (Apple on iOS, Google on Android).
  • Depending on your device and its settings, that recognition may happen on the device or on Apple's or Google's servers. In either case it is handled by the platform and governed by Apple's or Google's privacy policy — we, the developer, never receive, store, or transmit your voice audio or the transcribed text.
  • The resulting text is used only to run a dictionary search on your device.

Voice search is entirely optional; the app is fully usable by typing. The microphone is used for nothing else.

Audio pronunciation

Syriac pronunciations play from pre-recorded audio files bundled in the app. English and Arabic pronunciations use your device's built-in text-to-speech. Neither involves any data collection.

Data we collect

None. We do not collect analytics, usage statistics, crash logs, device identifiers, advertising identifiers, or any other data. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs in the app.

In-app purchases

The app offers optional "tips" to support development. These are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play); we never see or receive your payment information. Tipping is completely optional and unlocks no personal-data collection — it simply supports the work.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data at all, it is safe for users of any age, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone.

Fonts and content

The app bundles the Serto Urhoy font by Fr. Joseph Tarzi (Meltho font license, distributed with permission), Inter, and Noto Naskh Arabic (both SIL Open Font License 1.1). These are used for on-device rendering only and involve no data collection.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a new effective date.
