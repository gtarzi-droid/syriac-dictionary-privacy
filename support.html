Using the dictionary

Type a word or phrase in Syriac, English, or Arabic — the app detects the script automatically and shows translations in the other two languages, with transliteration, part of speech, root, and examples. You can override the detected language if needed.

Pronunciation

Tap the play button to hear a word or phrase. Syriac plays from a recorded clip when one is available (entries without a recording show a plain “no audio yet” note — never a wrong-language substitute). English and Arabic use your device's built-in speech. A global speed control lets you slow playback down.

Voice search

Tap the microphone to search by speaking in English or Arabic. Use the EN / ع toggle to pick the language. Syriac voice input does not exist on any platform yet, so there is no Syriac mic option — the app explains this where you'd expect the option to be.

Privacy

The app collects no data and works offline. See the Privacy Policy for details.
